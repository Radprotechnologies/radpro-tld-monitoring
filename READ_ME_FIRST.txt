Radpro TLD Monitoring - Multi Hospital Edition

Default logins:
1) Radpro Panel Admin: radpro / radpro123
2) Demo Hospital RSO: rso / rso123

Start on Windows:
1. Extract this ZIP fully to F:\RadproTLD or another normal folder.
2. Open the folder.
3. Double-click RUN_RADPRO.cmd.
4. Open http://localhost:3000

Do not open public/index.html directly.

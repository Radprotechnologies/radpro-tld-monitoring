Access model

Radpro Panel Admin: controls organization registration, trial, billing, package, renewal and hospital/user limits.

Organization Super Admin: manages all hospitals under one organization.

Hospital Admin / RSO: manages only their own hospital users, reports, forms, RSC and audit pack.

Employee: sees only personal TLD dose, forms, acknowledgements and notifications.
